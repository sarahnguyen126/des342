WHY OWN

  • Value

    Despite the high price, these CD players have great value for what they have to offer. They will give you unique experience that is worthy of the price.

  • Aesthetics

    These CD players offer a clean and modern look that brightens up the look of any room. Minimal and classy, just the right tool to display your CD collection.

  • Sound

    Imagine listening to your favorite songs off the sound that hit the wall. It will make you feel like you’re having a mini concert in your own room!

  • Atmosphere

    Possessing this type of CD player will make your room feel much more lively and interesting. Your room is always ready to play music!

  • Space-Saving

    Due to being wall-mounted, these CD players are unobtrusive and super neat for your cozy room. Saves you a great amount of space to put other items!

  • Versatile

    Did you know that these CD players can also turn into bluetooth speakers that let you connect to phones/laptops and play any song?
