In the vast kingdom of Daeho Fortress, there lives a guy name Jang-Uk. As the eldest son of the Jang family, he is the candidate for the next Gwanju position.

Due to his dazzling cold charms, Jang-Uk represents the Winter of the Four Seasons, a group of heirs of the most well-known mages families in Daeho.

With his passionate love for his lover, Mu-Deok, Jang-Uk wanted to find the Ice Stone to help her stay alive. However, in order to possess the Ice Stone, he must know how to use spells.

To be able to use spells, he must swallow poison, so that his gate of energy can be opened by Master Heo. Though dangerous, this is the only way to open his gate of energy...
